====== Hockey in the Greater Seattle Area ======
**Last Updated 8/12/11**

===== Major adult Ice Hockey leagues in the Seattle area=====
The major adult leagues are:

  * [[http://www.gshockey.com | the Greater Seattle Hockey League]] (Everett to Tacoma)

  * [[http://www.psaha.pointstreaksites.com/view/psaha/rainier-hockey-league | The Rainier hockey league]] (Tacoma)

  * [[http://www.cascadehockey.com/ | The Cascade Hockey League ]] (Lynnwood, Everett)

  * [[http://www.olyview.com/ | Olympic View Hockey League]] (Mountlake Terrace)

  * <del>[[http://eahl.net/ | Everett Adult Hockey League]] (Everett)</del> **Merged with the Cascade league**
